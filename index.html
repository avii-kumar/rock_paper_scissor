Rules

The basic rules of Rock, Paper, Scissors are simple:
  1. Players: The game is typically played between two players.
  2. Choices: Each player simultaneously forms one of three possible shapes with their hand:
    • Rock (a closed fist)
    • Paper (an open hand)
    • Scissors (a fist with the index and middle fingers extended, forming a V)
  3. Game Rules:
    • Rock beats Scissors (Rock crushes Scissors)
    • Scissors beats Paper (Scissors cut Paper)
    • Paper beats Rock (Paper covers Rock)
  4. Draw: If both players choose the same shape, the round is a draw.
  5. Objective: The goal is to select the option that defeats your opponent's choice. The player whose choice wins gets a point, and the game can be played for a set number of rounds or until one player reaches a specified number of points.
